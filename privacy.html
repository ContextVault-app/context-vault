3. How Conversation Text Is Processed

When you trigger a context extraction, the conversation text is sent from your browser to our Cloudflare Worker, which immediately forwards it to Anthropic's API for processing. The text is not stored on our servers at any point — it flows through in memory only.

Anthropic processes the text according to their own Privacy Policy. API usage data is subject to Anthropic's data retention policies (currently 30 days for API logs by default).

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, email REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_EMAIL with the subject "GDPR Request". We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

Deleting Your Data (Free Tier)

To delete your anonymous usage data, open the Context Vault extension settings and click "Delete My Data". This sends a deletion request to our Worker which removes your UUID and usage counter from Cloudflare KV immediately.

Deleting Your Data (Pro Subscribers)

Cancel your subscription via the Lemon Squeezy customer portal, then email us to request deletion of your license key cache. Lemon Squeezy retains transaction records for legal/tax purposes; contact them directly for those records.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • API keys are stored as encrypted secrets in Cloudflare Workers (never exposed to clients)
  • Cloudflare KV data is encrypted at rest
  • We do not store conversation content

However, no system is 100% secure. If you become aware of a security issue, please disclose it responsibly to REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_EMAIL.

7. Children's Privacy

Context Vault is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.
