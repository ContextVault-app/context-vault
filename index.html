FAQ

Common questions

Does Context Vault store my conversations?

No. Your conversation text is sent directly to our API for processing and is never stored. We only keep an anonymous usage counter to track your monthly free tier usage. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do I need an Anthropic API key?

No — not for either tier. We handle the API calls on our end. You just use the extension.

What counts as an "extraction"?

Each time you click "Extract Context" on a conversation counts as one extraction. Importing a conversation, viewing context cards, and copying handoff prompts are all free and unlimited.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your Lemon Squeezy customer portal. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked.

What happens if you shut down the service?

We'll give at least 30 days notice and issue prorated refunds to all active Pro subscribers. This is written into our Terms of Service.

Which AI platforms are supported?

Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT (chatgpt.com), and Gemini (gemini.google.com). We plan to add more based on user requests.
